Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Weather Quotes uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help us enhance your experience, provide core functionality, and show relevant content.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Needed to enable login and app functionality.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Cookies: Serve personalized ads (e.g., Google AdSense).
  • Social Login Cookies: Allow login through Google, Twitter, or Apple.

3. Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or reject cookies through our cookie banner. You may also clear cookies manually in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party services like Google AdSense and OAuth login providers may set their own cookies when used within our app.

5. Updates

We may update this policy as needed. Check this page regularly for changes.
