Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Weather Quotes collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and password (when signing up or logging in)
  • Usage data such as page views and quote interactions
  • Cookies for functionality, advertising, and analytics

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized content and quotes
  • To maintain your session and login state
  • To show relevant ads via Google AdSense and partners

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services including:

  • Google AdSense (personalized advertising)
  • Google Analytics (site usage tracking)
  • OAuth providers (Google, Twitter, Apple) for login

4. Your Choices

  • You can opt in or out of cookies using the cookie banner
  • You may request data deletion by contacting us
  • You can log out at any time to end your session

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at support@weatherquotes.com.
